Researchers aim to assess how different plant growth regulators (PGRs) influence the elongation of tomato plantlets grown in vitro.

  • Experimental units: 45 tomato plantlets cultured in identical sterile glass tubes with the same growth medium and light conditions.
  • Treatments (Factor): 3 PGR formulations:
    1. Auxin-dominant (IAA-based)
    2. Cytokinin-dominant (BAP-based)
    3. Balanced auxin‚Äìcytokinin mixture
  • Design: Each plantlet is randomly assigned to one of the three treatments (15 per group).
  • Response variable: Mean shoot length (cm) after 4 weeks.

Because all tubes are identical and conditions uniform, randomization alone ensures unbiased comparison ‚Äî a classic Completely Randomized Design.

üêÅ Example 2. Biomedical Experiment ‚Äî Analgesic Doses in Genetically Identical Mice

A pharmacology lab evaluates the effect of different doses of a new analgesic on the pain threshold of laboratory mice.

  • Experimental units: 40 male mice of the same inbred strain, all 10 weeks old, maintained under identical housing, feeding, and lighting conditions.
  • Treatments (Factor): 4 doses of the analgesic:
    1. 0 mg/kg (control)
    2. 2 mg/kg
    3. 5 mg/kg
    4. 10 mg/kg
  • Design: Each mouse is randomly assigned to one of the four doses (10 per treatment).
  • Response variable: Pain response latency (seconds) measured in a hot-plate test after drug administration.

Since the mice are genetically and environmentally homogeneous, no blocking is required ‚Äî randomization alone provides a valid CRD structure.
